  • Gold & Diamonds Purchase Plan This is a scheme that provides free making charges. Making charge is free on items that attract value addition up to 15%. It is aimed at providing easy possession of gold for the customers. Gold can be purchased opting any of the seven slabs of Rs. 500, 1000, 2000, 5000, 10000, 20000 and 25000 on advance equal monthly installments for 11 months. Gold is returned in the 12th month after equal monthly payment for 11 consecutive months for the value of amount paid. The highest attraction is that no making charge/value addition is made on items attracting making charges up to 15%. In other words the customers are given ornaments for the entire amount paid without any making charges for items that attract making charges up to 15%. Those items on which making charges exceed 15%, value addition is limited to the percentage exceeding 15% only.
